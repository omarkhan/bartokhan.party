We're getting married (again)

16 June 2018

Party people:

We’ve hit a few snags with our house rental - turns out there are more rules than we expected. What we thought was the backyard of the house is actually national park territory so the layout of the party will be a bit different: we won’t be able to have many chairs and tables outside so it’ll be more of a picnic-type arrangement for dinner; there’s always the option to eat inside at a table.

Parking is very limited, so we kindly ask whoever can to carpool or take taxis/lyfts/ubers. There is public parking next door at the national park services building, as well as a half mile down the road at the Boston Store visitor center, but parking spaces are not guaranteed. If you need to park elsewhere give us a call and we can arrange for someone to come pick you up (please no later than 7!). For those that are parking overnight, we will provide a hangtag that you’ll need to display.

Damn the man, save the party. It’s going to be excellent.

Allison & Omar's Wedding Party
